Preparing for Coaching Sessions üìù

Make the most of your 20-minute coaching slot by coming prepared!

‚úÖ Before the Session

  • ‚úì Update your project board - All tasks and progress must be visible
  • ‚úì Prepare a brief pitch covering:
    • Work completed since last session
    • Plans for next sprint
    • Key decisions made
    • Current blockers
  • ‚úì Bring specific questions - Not vague requests like "help us with X"

üí° During the Session

  • ‚Üí Be ready to explain your technical choices
  • ‚Üí Defend your decisions with evidence and reasoning
  • ‚Üí Show, don't tell - Demo your work when possible
  • ‚Üí Take notes on feedback and action items

‚ùå What NOT to Do

  • ‚ùå Come unprepared without reviewing your progress
  • ‚ùå Ask coaches to solve problems you haven't researched
  • ‚ùå Make excuses for lack of progress
  • ‚ùå Ignore previous coaching feedback
